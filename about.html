About Emerging Languages

In 2010, Emerging Languages was held as for the first time as an informal "camp"-style event. Space was generously provided by O'Reilly during their annual OSCON conference. Our event was popular enough that O’Reilly agreed to make it a formal part of OSCON that year.

In 2012 Emerging Languages joined up with the Strange Loop conference in St Louis. We're continuing on in conjunction with Strange Loop in 2013.

Thanks

Appreciation is due to the following folks who have helped in various ways to make our event happen over the years: Brady Forrest, Alex Miller, Steve Jenson, Michael Fogus, and all the volunteers at OSCON at Strange Loop.
